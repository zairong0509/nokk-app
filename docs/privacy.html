Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. About the App

NOKK is a home safety voice app that helps people who are alone at home by playing protective voices in various situations.

2. Data Collection and Use

NOKK does NOT collect any personal information.

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any user personal information, and has the following characteristics:

  • No registration required
  • No login required
  • No personal information input required
  • No communication with external servers (completely offline)

3. Local Storage Data

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally:

  • Language settings (Korean, English, Japanese, Spanish)
  • Theme settings (Light mode, Dark mode)
  • Voice tone selection (Normal, Firm, Angry)
  • Quick action button settings
  • Premium subscription status (payment info managed by Apple/Google)

4. Permissions

This app may use the following permissions:

  • Audio playback: Required to play protective voices.
  • Internet (optional): Only used for premium subscription payments.

These permissions are used solely to provide the app's core functionality and do not collect any personal information.

5. Advertising

This app displays banner ads to free users through Google AdMob.

  • AdMob may collect anonymized advertising identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve ads.
  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.) is collected.
  • Premium users do not see any ads.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Advertising (for free users)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Premium subscription payment processing (optional)

Payment information is managed by Apple or Google, and NOKK does not have access to payment information.

7. Data Security

All settings data is stored only within the user's device and is not transmitted externally. When you delete the app, all data is deleted along with it.

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not collect personal information from users of any age, so no special measures for children's privacy are required. However, the app is designed to be used by children when they are home alone.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be modified to reflect changes in laws or services. If there are changes, they will be announced through this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@nokk.app
