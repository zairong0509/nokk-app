# Privacy Policy for NOKK

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

NOKK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application NOKK ("App").

## Summary

**NOKK does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** The app is designed to work completely offline and does not require any personal information to function.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Browsing history
- Contact information
- Photos or media files
- Health or fitness data

## How the App Works

### Offline Functionality
All voice audio files are stored locally on your device. The app does not require an internet connection to play safety voices.

### Optional Google Sign-In
Google Sign-In is completely optional and not required to use the app. If you choose to sign in:
- We only receive your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)
- This information is stored locally on your device only
- We do not transmit this data to any server
- You can sign out at any time to remove this information

### In-App Purchases
Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment information.

### Advertisements
The free version of NOKK displays banner advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymized data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

For Google's privacy practices, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage

All app data (preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob (Free Version Only)
- Purpose: Display advertisements
- Data collected: Anonymized ad interaction data
- Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple App Store / Google Play Store
- Purpose: Handle in-app purchases
- Data collected: Transaction data (by Apple/Google, not us)
- Privacy policies:
  - Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  - Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

NOKK is designed as a safety app and may be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to:
- Access
- Correct
- Delete
- Export

Your app preferences can be reset by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the minimal data stored locally on your device:
- Secure local storage
- No data transmission
- No external databases

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
